Prison Rideshare Concept
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
When I was unemployed a few years ago, I decided to take on self employment and find contract projects out there to make some extra finances. One of the projects that came up was an idea about having a system where
families of prisoners in jails far away could connect with drivers willing to go to the prisons near them and transport these families there to see their loved ones.
This has been generalized in this project to allow people to seek out willing drivers to move them from one location to another. I don't believe that it is much different from other rideshare applications,
and I don't believe that the architecture is not readily available on the internet. I am not sure if my friend who initially wanted this project understood the scope of it wad the necessary resources to 
handle such a thing. He had no capital and was expecting me to do it for FREE. This projet in GitHub is just a reflection of those ideas and a sandbox to play and learn about Django and Python, and whatever
Geocoding type services out there. I imaging that this will require some Apple and Android coding as well.
